CBZ Savings Account offers flexible,everyday banking with unlimited deposits and withdrawals,low service fees, and convinient access via ATM, branch, online and mobile banking. Ideal for individuals and business, it provides essentials features like account alerts,checkbooks, and secure transactions for easy money management.

Offers a convinient and safe method of making payments. The ATM offers an alternative method of accessing cash.

The CBZ Account has the following features and benefits:

  • Access to account around the clock through CBZ Touch App and Internet Banking
  • Cheque book issued with crossed cheques
  • Cheque Guarantee Card,available on application
  • ATM card for use on CBZ and Zimswitch Network
  • No minimum balance
  • An array of payment options by cheque,Debit Card or Inter-Bank Tranfer
